Financial Analysis of Banks using Tableau

Welcome to our project, "Financial Analysis of Banks using Tableau"!

As a group of enthusiastic college students, we embarked on this endeavor to explore the world of finance and data visualization using Tableau.

Our goal was to analyze and present financial data of various banks in an intuitive and interactive manner, allowing users to gain insights into the performance and trends of these institutions.

By leveraging the power of Tableau, we were able to transform raw financial data into visually compelling graphs, charts, and dashboards. Through our efforts, we aimed to provide a user-friendly platform that enables individuals, investors, and financial professionals to explore and evaluate the financial health of banks with ease.
